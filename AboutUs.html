About Hive Connect

Hive Connect is your all-in-one platform for discovering, tracking, and celebrating volunteer work. Designed with students in mind, our app makes it easy to log hours, explore local opportunities, and stay organized—all while making a real impact in your community.

Whether you're fulfilling school requirements, building your resume, or simply giving back, Hive Connect helps you stay connected, motivated, and recognized for your efforts.

We believe volunteering should be rewarding, accessible, and fun—and we’re here to make that happen.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Prairie View students to engage meaningfully with their Houston community. We make it simple to find volunteer events, track your hours, and reflect on your impact—so every act of service counts. We aim to make tracking volunteering hours as a student more accessible, fun, and helpful — every event, every time.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student can easily connect with causes they care about. A future where schools, nonprofits, and volunteers collaborate seamlessly—and where every hour of service is valued and visible. Hive Connect is building that future, one event at a time.

Our Future Goals

We’re working to expand Hive Connect with smarter tools for event discovery, hour verification, and school integration.

Soon, you’ll be able to earn badges, share your impact stories, and even get matched with opportunities based on your interests and availability.

Our goal is to make volunteering not just easier—but more meaningful, personalized, and celebrated.
